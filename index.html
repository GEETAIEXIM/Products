Our Raisins

Malayar Raisins

Malayar Raisins are known for their unique sweetness and texture. They come in three premium varieties that cater to different tastes and preferences.

  • A: A basic variety with a mild, sweet taste, perfect for everyday snacking.
  • AA: A higher quality raisin with a deeper flavor and more succulent texture.
  • AAA: The top-tier variety, offering the sweetest and juiciest raisins with a tender bite.

Golden Raisins

Sweet and juicy golden raisins, perfect for snacking or baking. Rich in vitamins and minerals, they provide a healthy treat for any occasion.

  • Golden Sangli AAA: The best quality, naturally sweet and tender.
  • Golden Sangli Long: A longer variant, sweet with a slightly tangy taste.

Black Raisins

Rich in flavor and packed with nutrients, black raisins are a great addition to any diet. Known for their antioxidant properties, they support overall health.

  • Dried Black: These raisins are dried to perfection, offering a deep, intense flavor and a chewy texture.

Green Raisins

Tangy and delicious, green raisins offer a unique taste. They are a great source of fiber and are known for their digestive benefits.

  • Long Green: A long variety of green raisins with a mild tartness, ideal for snacking or adding to dishes.
